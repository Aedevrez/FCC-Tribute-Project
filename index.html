Brandon Sanderson

Maker of his own universe

His Life and Works

  • Born in December 1975 in Lincoln, Nebraska.
  • Enrolled at Brigham Young University as a Biochemistry Major in 1994.
  • Went to Seoul, Korea as a missionary from 1995 to 1997.
  • Became an English major when he returned BYU.
  • Volunteered for The Leading Edge, BYU’s science fiction/fantasy magazine.
  • Took a class from David Farland, author of the popular Runelords series.
  • His first book, Elantris, was published in 2005.
  • Mistborn Trilogy, Alloy of Law and Warbreaker are published.
  • Married Emily Bushman in July 2006.
  • Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians, a middle-grade novel, was published in October 2007.
  • Was chosen by Harriet McDougal Rigney to complete Robert Jordan’s Wheel of Time series after his untimely passing in December 2007. 2009’s The Gathering Storm and 2010’s Towers of Midnight was followed by the final volume in the series, A Memory of Light, in January 2013.
